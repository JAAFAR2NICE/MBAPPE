His Story in PSG

I arrived in Paris at 10:30 p.m. one night. When I arrived, I called Antero [Henrique, PSG sporting director] to inform him of my arrival. Mbappé scored twice in the final 10 minutes of PSG's 3-1 Ligue 1 win over Guingamp in his first appearance of the season. The following league game, the front three of Mbappé, Cavani, and Neymar all scored in a 3-1 win over Angers at home, with Mbappé scoring on a volley and setting up Neymar to score the third. On September 1, he scored and assisted in a 4-2 away win over Nîmes, but was sent off for the first time in his career in injury time after shoving Téji Savanier in retaliation for a late challenge from behind, for which Savanier was also sent off. Mbappé told reporters after the match about his dismissal: "If I could do it all over again, I would. I apologize to the supporters and to everyone, but this is unacceptable to me." Mbappé became the youngest player (19 years and 9 months) to score four goals in a 5-0 home victory over Lyon on October 8th.

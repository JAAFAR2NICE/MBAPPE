About

Kylian Mbappé Lottin (born December 20, 1998) is a French footballer who plays as a forward for Ligue 1 club Paris Saint-Germain and the France national team. Mbappé, regarded as one of the best players in the world, is known for his dribbling abilities, exceptional speed, and finishing. Mbappé, who was born in Paris and raised in nearby Bondy, began his senior club career in 2015 with Monaco, where he won the Ligue 1 title. Mbappé, then 18, signed for Paris Saint-Germain on an eventual permanent transfer worth €180 million in 2017, making him the club's second-most expensive player and the most expensive teenager.He won four Ligue 1 titles and three Coupes de France while at the club, and he is the club's second-highest all-time goalscorer.

Mbappé made his senior debut for France in 2017, when he was 18 years old. Mbappé became the youngest French player to score in a World Cup at the 2018 FIFA World Cup, and the second teenager, after Pelé, to score in a World Cup Final. He finished the tournament as France's joint second-highest goalscorer, and he was named FIFA World Cup Best Young Player and French Player of the Year for his efforts.
